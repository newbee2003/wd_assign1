The people - The heart of the city

Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam, is a wonderful city with good food and people.

Vietnam has been a popular place for tourism, however, the image of its people toward foreigners are not that good because of the scammers. Though these two photos are a small aspect of the people in Hanoi I want to change the existed image about Vietnamese in some people by art.

The beautiful drawing from an old artist. This kind of drawing has always been a part of the culture around Guom Lake in Hanoi.

Another group is a small band that was playing on Guom Lake. Though the song may only reach a small number of people at the time and it had indeed lightened up the mood around that area.

The Scenery

Hanoi has a lot of beautiful scenery but the most popular one is probably Guom Lake.

The photo on the left may be only a tiny view of a gigantic lake, but it has already shown us the beauty of it. The green tree alongside is blue sky and water - such an impressive view.
