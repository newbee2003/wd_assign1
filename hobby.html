Photography

Photography has been always a part of my life. However, my life as a photographer only started when I started grade 10 and joined the Photography club in my high school.

Taking pictures is not only fun, but it also helps you to see closer to details that you normally forgot: the light, the expressions of people, etc.

Coding

Many people would think that your job can't be your hobby.

However, to me, that sentence is completely wrong. I chose to study programming to make it into a career and I still find joy in coding. Of course, coding can be hard and frustrated at the time, but the feeling of finishing a project or a product is priceless.

As a person who majored in Maths, I enjoy solving problems, however, Maths can be unrealistic. To me, programming is using Maths, using logic to solve problems in the world.

Reading

Books are knowledge that is given to us by our ancestors. Some of you may say that fictional books can not give you knowledge.

However, every book has its own purpose from teaching to telling us the story from the author's eyes. Understanding new things are important but you can not forget about learning about human.

Seeing the world from other people's eyes can help enrich your point of view and help you grow further as a person.

I will share with you one of my favourite books: The Count of Monte Cristo. This may be not the most meaningful book, and Edmon Dantes is not the best character. However, to me, that is the story that I enjoy, and make me curious about the world. What has the author been through or seen to create a fictional world that cruel and detailed?

Listening to Music

Listening to music is probably has a different meaning to every person. Some may find music to be the thing that helps them during hardship. Some may only listen to music for relaxation. Others may listen to Mozart to improve their efficiency in learning.

I am the second type. Listening to music brings me joy and just like books, music can show you the musician's point of view. I enjoy the complexity of classical music though I am still not good enough to fully understand them. My favourite music change from day to day so currently I mostly listen to Japanese music especially from Yoasobi. Some of his works that I enjoy are:

  • 優しい彗星
  • 怪物
  • あの夢をなぞって
  • Probably
